Heritage Box

A curated cultural product celebrating local craft and stories.

Explore the Box

Story

Heritage Box was created to connect modern life with timeless traditions. Each box contains handcrafted items sourced from master artisans, along with a booklet telling the stories behind the craft and the people who make it.

Cultural Background

The designs and materials are inspired by regional patterns and ancient techniques passed down through generations. We work directly with communities to ensure cultural respect, fair pay, and sustainable practices.

Features

  • Handmade artisan products
  • Story booklet and provenance
  • Sustainable packaging
  • Limited-run seasonal themes

Scenarios

Perfect as a thoughtful gift, a home accent, or an educational tool for workshops and cultural events. Use it to inspire conversations and support craft economies.

Details

What's inside

Each box includes 3–5 handcrafted items, a story booklet, and care instructions. Items vary seasonally.

Dimensions & Materials

Carefully packaged with recyclable materials. Items are made from wood, textile, clay, and natural dyes.
