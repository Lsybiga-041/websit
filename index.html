故事Story

三百年前，祂在金門前線迎風而立，用石雕的身軀為居民擋下漫天風沙、驅邪避凶。三百年後，我們將風獅爺以當代設計語彙重新演繹，讓守護不只是一種信仰，而是日常的力量。 Three hundred years ago the guardian lion stood against the winds of Kinmen. Today we reinterpret that guardian as a contemporary desktop collectible, blending tradition with modern design.

文化背景Cultural Background

風獅爺散落在金門各村落，守護著人們的生活與記憶。產品保留傳統符碼，同時尊重工藝來源與在地社群。 Guardian lions dot villages across Kinmen, protecting lives and memories. Our product preserves traditional motifs while honoring craft origins and local communities.
